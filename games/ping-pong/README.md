## Description

 - Every time the ball touches the bars the score will get incremented by 
one.
 - In case the ball fails to touch the bars the score will get reset to 
zero '0'.


## Key Bindings

<p> press <code> <kbd>A</kbd> </code> to move the rods to the <code> 
<b>LEFT</b> </code> direction </p>
<p> press <code> <kbd>D</kbd> </code> to move the rods to the <code> 
<b>RIGHT</b> </code> direction </p>
